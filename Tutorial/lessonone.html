Welcome to the Solomon Project's tutorial levels.
This tutorial will not show you all you need to go through the main story.
It is only to teach you how to play, how to think and how to look for hints.

Your main task is always to find the URL of the next level.
The URL is the address bar you see on the top of your screen (which says solomonproject.netlify.app/tutorial/lessonone.htm)

Any way [except for cheats] could be the correct one, so try to do everything you think of.

This lesson is the easiest and the most popular challenge at the beginning.
Look at the picture, look at the title - then look at the URL.
