An image can be just a decorative element. Sometimes, however, it may be the key you are looking for.
There are many ways to encode password or clues in a picture.
Image editors, such as free ones on pixlr.com, can help you make adjustments to a picture, such as adjusting brightness, contrast, saturation (nothing too difficult!).
Not all levels require such editing. You may ignore the photo by default,
but should you ever feel like you are stuck despite having tried everything, simple image editing might help.
